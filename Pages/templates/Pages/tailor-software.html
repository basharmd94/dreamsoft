What Is Tailor Shop Management Software?

Tailor Shop Software is a specialized tool for clothing and tailoring businesses, designed to automate order management, inventory tracking, and billing. DreamSoft POS helps you manage customer orders with detailed measurements, fabric types, and delivery dates — all in one platform.

  • Order Management

    Track each customer order from intake to delivery with status updates and deadline reminders.

  • Inventory Control

    Monitor fabrics, threads, buttons, and other materials in real-time with low-stock alerts.

  • Billing & Invoicing

    Auto-generate invoices and accept mobile payments like bKash, Nagad, cards, and cash.

  • Customer Database

    Store measurements and preferences to provide personalized services and encourage repeat orders.

  • Staff & Workflow Management

    Assign orders to staff members and track their progress for improved productivity.
