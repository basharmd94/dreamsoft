Understanding POS

What is POS Software and How Does It Work?

A Point of Sale (POS) system is a digital platform used by businesses to process sales transactions, manage inventory, and track customer purchases.

Modern POS software in Bangladesh does much more than simple billing. It integrates:

  • Inventory management
  • Customer relationship management (CRM)
  • Payment processing
  • Financial reporting
  • Sales analytics

With DreamSoft POS, every sale automatically updates your inventory, records customer transactions, and generates financial reports in real time. This allows business owners to monitor operations, increase efficiency, and improve profitability.
