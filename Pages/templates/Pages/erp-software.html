Understanding ERP

What is an ERP System?

ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) is a business management software that integrates all core processes of an organization into one centralized system.

A modern ERP system in Bangladesh connects departments such as:

  • Finance & HR
  • Manufacturing & Sales
  • Inventory & Procurement
  • Supply Chain Management

ERP systems help companies automate manual tasks, reduce errors, improve productivity, gain business insights, and scale operations efficiently. Whether you operate a small business or a large enterprise, ERP software is essential for managing modern businesses.
