What is an ERP System?

ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) software is a unified platform that manages and automates a company's core business processes. By replacing isolated tools with a centralized system, an ERP connects your finance, HR, manufacturing, and supply chain data in real time. This ensures 100% data accuracy, lowers operational costs, and gives decision-makers the visibility needed to scale efficiently.
