How DreamSoft Restaurant POS Works

Our POS software integrates all restaurant operations into a single platform, improving efficiency, accuracy, and management visibility.

Order Management & Tracking

Table, takeaway, and online orders managed seamlessly. Orders are sent directly to the kitchen display (KOT system) to eliminate errors and speed up preparation.

Billing & Invoice Generation

Automatic, VAT/GST-compliant invoices generated instantly. Faster billing reduces customer wait times and eliminates manual calculation errors.

Kitchen Display Integration

Real-time order updates flow directly to kitchen screens, helping chefs prepare dishes accurately and quickly — no more paper slips lost in translation.

Real-Time Reports & Analytics
