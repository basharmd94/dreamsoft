Understanding Inventory

What is Inventory Management?

Inventory management is the process of tracking, organizing, and controlling stock levels within a business. It includes monitoring raw materials, finished goods, and products stored in warehouses or retail locations.

Effective inventory management ensures businesses always have the right products available at the right time. It prevents overstocking and stock shortages while improving cash flow and operational efficiency.

Using modern inventory management software in Bangladesh allows businesses to automate stock tracking, manage product movement, and generate reports for better decision making.
